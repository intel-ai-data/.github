## 🧠 About Us

**Intel AI** is a data collection and AI solutions company dedicated to accelerating the adoption of Artificial Intelligence across industries.

We believe that the future of AI depends on **high-quality, diverse, and well-structured datasets**. Our mission is to provide organizations with reliable data services that fuel the development of accurate, ethical, and impactful AI models.

At Intel AI, we combine **innovation, precision, and scalability** to help businesses transform complex challenges into smarter solutions.
